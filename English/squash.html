About Tuborg Squash

It tastes wonderfully refreshing of orange. A real one classic which was introduced back in 1936.
Tuborg Squash tastes like a good Danish orange water must taste. Well supported by humorous advertisements Tuborg Squash has become a charming folk property across generations.

ingredients

Water, orange juice from concentrate (5.8%), carbon dioxide, acid (E330), sweeteners (E951, E950), preservative (E202), orange oils, carrot juice, antioxidant (E300)), stabilizer (E410). Contains a source of phenylalanine

Allergens:

Preservative (E202), orange oils, carrot juice, antioxidant (ascorbic acid), stabilizer (carob seed flour).
